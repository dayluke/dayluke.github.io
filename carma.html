Carma

An online multiplayer vehicle combat game.

Carma is a casual multiplayer game based on fast and hectic vehicle combat with the addition of many power-ups and killstreaks. Each match is set on a miniature planet, with different planets having different environments which pose unique challenges and opportunities to players.
