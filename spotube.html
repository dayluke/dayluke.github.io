Spotube

A chrome extension for Spotify.

Spotube is a useful tool for easily adding YouTube songs to your Spotify playlists, in just two clicks. At first, it was made solely for myself, to save me time everytime I heard a song I liked on YouTube. However, later I realised that this tool could be utilised by people with similar desires.

    Project Details
  • My first Chrome Extension
  • 30+ installs in first week
  • Over 500 installs in total
  • Also compatible with Firefox
  • 5 star rating on Chrome Store
  • Uses OAuth2.0 for Spotify Authentication
