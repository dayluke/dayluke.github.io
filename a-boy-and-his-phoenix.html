A Boy and His Phoenix

An indie puzzle game.

A Boy and His Phoenix is a casual puzzle game, made as my submission for the Falmouth CCC 'Phoenix' game jam. You work your way through progressively difficult levels, helping the boy and his phoenix get to safety.

    Project Details
  • Winner of the Falmouth CCC Game Jam
  • Made single-handedly
  • Developed in Unity (C#)
  • 1-month development time
