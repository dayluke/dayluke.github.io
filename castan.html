Castan

A post-apocalyptic roguelike, looter shooter, FPS game.

Castan is set in a large post-apocalyptic environment, filled with overgrown vegetation and foliage. Over 5 days, you have to shoot and loot your way through mutated, dangerous creatures to gather enough money to pay off your debt to the mafia. Through speaking to the various NPCs located around the map, you will discover a rich backstory that ties the elements of the game together.
