Hungry Shark Primal

An action-packed mobile game.

Hungry Shark Primal is an action-packed mobile adventure game and the latest installment in the award-winning Hungry Shark franchise. Set in prehistoric times, it offers players a chance to explore an ancient era teeming with prehistoric creatures, such as dinosaurs, ancient fish, reptiles, cavemen, and of course, prehistoric sharks.
